ABOUT ME

I'm Matt.

I'm a freelance software developer, web designer and analyst. I live in Indianapolis, Indiana with my wife and daughter.

I've been fascinated by computers since my father brought an x8086 machine home from work in 1983. I wrote my first text-based game a year later. I built Bulletin Board Systems on dial up modems in High School and the first languages I learned were GW Basic and Turbo Pascal. I've been fortunate to be alive during the first generation of humans for whom personal computational devices are a part of our whole life. The opportunity to train at Eleven 50 Academy is an opportunity to re-ignite a lifelong interest, and bring my skills up to a most modern standard.

My specialty is web development. I use Javascript, HTML and CSS to create rich, interactive and functional web applications. Some examples include:

  • Design highly effective sales funnels to convert your visitors to customers.
  • Clean, intuitive and secure web interfaces between your clients and your data.
  • Fully custom browser based applications to access and manipulate external data sources.

I have experience in data analysis with a concentration in financial and operational data. I can help you use your data in new ways to:

  • Find patterns in your operations to emperically support decision making.
  • Extract meta data from your unique data and format the insights in simple, meaningful presentations.
  • Construct architectures and algorithms to store, access and make use of non-standard, complex datasets.

Finally, I am able to use a broad array of design software to develop media or support designers. I support design by:

  • Scripting and Automating operations in many Creative Cloud products.
  • Converting design ideas to web, print and broadcast ready formats.
